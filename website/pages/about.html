About BookShop

Since its founding in 1997, BookShop has sold millions of quality book copies to satisfied customers in Singapore. We operate physical stores in Bugis, Orchard, and Little India. For your convenience, you can shop online to get books delivered straight to your doorstep.

Our Story

BookShop was born from a passion for reading and a commitment to making books accessible to all Singaporeans. Over the years, we have grown alongside the evolving landscape of bookstores in Singapore, a city with a rich literary heritage dating back over a century. Inspired by the legacy of iconic bookstores such as Popular and Kinokuniya, we strive to blend tradition with innovation to serve our diverse community of readers.

Our Vision

To be Singapore’s most beloved and trusted bookstore, fostering a vibrant reading culture that inspires lifelong learning and imagination.

Our Mission

  • To provide a wide and carefully curated selection of books across all genres and languages to meet the needs of every reader.
  • To create welcoming physical spaces where book lovers can explore, discover, and connect.
  • To embrace digital transformation by offering a seamless online shopping experience that complements our brick-and-mortar stores.
  • To support local authors and promote Singapore’s literary talents alongside international bestsellers.
  • To host engaging events and activities that nurture a community passionate about books and knowledge.

Why Choose BookShop

With decades of experience serving Singapore’s readers, BookShop combines the charm of traditional bookstores with the convenience of modern technology. Whether you visit us in-store or shop online, you can expect expert recommendations, excellent customer service, and a commitment to quality.

Join us as we continue to write new chapters in Singapore’s rich bookstore story, making books accessible and enjoyable for generations to come.
