Book Description

A charming collection of black and white drawings and handwritten narratives that depict short stories from Boey's childhood growing up in Malaysia. This graphic novel is filled with stories of surviving school, siblings, and parents from a time when the world was seen simply as black and white.

Best known for his art on coffee cups, artist Boey collects a series of prose and cartoon recollections of his childhood in Malaysia, originally posted on his popular blog, iamboey.com.

About the Author

Cheeming Boey (born 12 April 1978) is a Malaysian artist. Primarily known only by his last name Boey, he is best recognised by his illustrations on foam coffee cups, his daily webcomic "I Am Boey", and his autobiographical graphic novel "When I Was A Kid".
